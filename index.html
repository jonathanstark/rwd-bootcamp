What is Rabble?

Rabble is a fun, easy way to let the folks on your site talk to each other.

Why Use Rabble?

  • User Engagement

    Adding Rabble to your web site is a great way to engage your users and encourage them to spend more time on your site discussing articles, stories, and more.

    Learn More »

  • Flexible and Open

    Customize the default Rabble client with your own CSS, or build a brand new client on top of the Rabble Client API. The possibilities are endless.

    Customizing »

  • Simple Integration

    Getting started with Rabble is a piece of cake. Simply copy and paste a few short lines to include the provided JavaScript and CSS files on your site.
